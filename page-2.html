    • Dr House

    Dr Gregory House, head of the country's only diagnostic service, is described as arrogant, cynical, unconventional and asocial. He limps due to chronic pain in his right leg and abuses Vicodin for pain relief. With his team, he is tasked with unraveling medical mysteries when other doctors have been unable to find the cause or treatment of the symptoms that the patients present. He must also give consultations open to the public, consultations whose number and frequency are often the subject of negotiations between House and Cuddy his director, the former not keen to waste his precious time on too simple cases. Often in conflict with his director, hospital administrator and dean of faculty, Dr. Lisa Cuddy, medically as well as hierarchically or personally, he relies on the fluctuating support of his best and only friend, the Dr James Wilson.


    Medical series, Dramatic comedy (2004/2012)
    Created by David Shore
