    • Lucifer

    Tired and tired of being the "Lord of the Underworld", Lucifer Morningstar abandons his kingdom and goes to Los Angeles where he owns a nightclub called "Lux". Lucifer received the gift of forcing people to reveal their deepest desires. One evening, Lucifer witnesses the murder of a pop singer in front of his club. He therefore decides to go in search of the culprit and crosses his path a policewoman named Chloe Decker who resists her donation and puts a strain on him. While Lucifer Morningstar and Chloe Decker team up to find the murderer, God sends the angel Amenadiel to Earth to convince Lucifer to rule Hell again.

    Thriller, Comedy (2016/2020)

    Created by Tom Kapinos
